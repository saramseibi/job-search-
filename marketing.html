Marketing Manager

Tunis Full Time 800DT - 1200DT

Job description

Splio is a Martech scale-up based in Paris that has completed three acquisitions between 2018 and 2021. The company has 250 employees, including 90 in product and R&D, and 5 offices in Europe and MEA. Mireille Messine is the CEO of Splio. Splio develops a SaaS platform for Individuation® Marketing targeting B2C marketing professionals. With a portfolio of more than 500 brands ranging from medium-sized businesses to large groups in four key industries (Retail, e-commerce, food service, and telecommunications), notable clients include Longchamp, Decathlon, Rue du Commerce, Nicolas, Go Sport, Cojean, Brioche Dorée, and Orange. Splio is evolving Marketing Automation towards Individuation® Marketing to allow companies to activate and engage each of their customers on a case-by-case basis in a unique relationship thanks to AI. The strength of Splio lies in the combination of technology enthusiasts and marketing experts.

Responsibility

Splio is looking for motivated, curious, internationally-minded individuals passionate about tech and digital marketing, who are ready to take on the daily challenges of an ambitious scale-up conquering Europe and MEA. Regardless of the position, all contribute to the company's growth dynamics. Energy, enthusiasm, and initiative are fundamental aspects of the Splio team's character.

Profile sought

  • Master programming languages HTML/CSS/JS
  • Master Responsive Design, graphic design, Photoshop, visual design
  • Master the Office Suite
  • Master professional English
