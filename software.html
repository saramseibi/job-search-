Job description

Joining iObeya means participating in a rapidly growing business project in France and internationally. It means working in a dynamic environment and a participative structure where everyone invests in the development of the company's life. In 10 years, iObeya's Digital Visual Management solutions have already won over more than 400 large companies from all industries, in over 30 countries, and 500,000 daily users. Based on Lean and Agile practices, iObeya revolutionizes performance management in all departments of the company by giving leaders, managers, and operators real-time visual cockpit. Today, our goal is to become the international leader in Digital Visual Management.

Responsibility

iObeya offers equal opportunities and is committed to providing a safe and fair working environment. We value honesty, transparency, teamwork, and a philosophy that the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. We focus on people, collaboration, and enthusiasm: all team members share in the success of the company regardless of their position or title.

Profile sought

  • Java 17, Spring Boot, Quarkus, Hibernate, NodeJS
  • Typescript, Vue.js
  • You are a computer engineer with a Master's degree (Bac+5)
  • You have 2 to 5 years of experience.
